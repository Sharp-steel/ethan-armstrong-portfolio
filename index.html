I'm a current game designer with a strong passion for design, game creation and enjoy all things video games. My work is inspired and driven by culture, adventure, excitement, and innovation.

About Ethan Armstrong

After deliberating about my future for a long time, I finally discovered my passion for game design. I specialize in worldbuilding and narratives, and have used my prior design experience through graphic design and UI/UX design in order to create RPG style games, with an emphasis on fantasy, sci-fi and medieval themes that have a very unique feel to them, although I have also dabbled in other genres as well.


Most of my personal work and projects are inspired from my culture, personal hobbies, and favourite games. Despite hailing from Montreal, my English heritage has always allowed and inspired me to embrace fantasy, sci-fi and medieval culture within games. Most of the games that I play and make for this reason are usually consistent with these genres and subgenres.


Currently, I am a student at Concordia University in the Computation Arts Specialization program. I also hold prior experience as a graphic designer, graduating from the Graphic and Web Design program at John Abbott College.
